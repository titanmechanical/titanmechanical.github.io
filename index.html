About Us

Titan Mechanical is a full-service automotive repair shop specialising in diesel performance tuning and diagnostics. We use advanced Snap-On diagnostic tools and dyno testing to identify and fix issues.

We handle general maintenance, logbook servicing, and a wide range of repairs to keep your vehicle running efficiently.
